Tours

Find out our current pleasant tours below.

Totally Tudor

A lighthearted look at life in Tudor Leicester. The tour begins at the Old Grammar School, Highcross Street and ends at the Newarke Houses Museum. The tour lasts approximately 90 minutes where you can hear about Tudor:

* Entertainment & Education
* Sayings & Superstitions
* Sentences & Sanctuary
* Fiesty Friars
* House & Home
* Celebrated Citizens and much more

Hidden History of Wigston Magna

The tour begins at St Wistan’s Church, Bull Head Street, Wigston Magna and lasts for approximately 90 minutes. Take a walk through the medieval lanes and hear about the surprising history of Wigston Magna including:

* Wigston’s Highwayman
* Baggy Marsh
* Squire’s Knob
* Medieval Medicine and much more

Kilby Bridge – History and Nature Walk

The tour begins outside the Navigation Inn, Kilby Bridge and lasts for approximately 90 minutes. Enjoy a stroll by the canal in the hamlet of Kilby Bridge and hear about:

* Canal Life
* Plant Life
* Folklore
* The Drinking Ritual
* Witch Trials
* The Heavenly Hand and much more

The History of Aylestone Village

The tour begins at Aylestone Park, Aylestone Road, Aylestone and lasts for approximately 90 minutes. An off the beaten track tour through old Aylestone. Discover the fascinating history of Aylestone and hear about:

* Boozy Bell Ringers
* Royal Connections
* The Body Snatcher
* Legendary Love
* The Ghost of the Woods and much more

The Newarke and Castle Area

The tour begins at the Magazine and lasts for approximately 90 minutes. A guided walk around the beautiful Newarke and Castle area. Stories include:

* Missing Bones
* Tudor Spanko
* The Secret Garden
* Exquisite Earls
* Tales of Myth and Legend

Tales from the Dark Side –Wigston Magna

The tour begins at All Saints Church, Bushloe End, Wigston Magna. New for 2016 this 90 minutes guided walk introduces you to the darker side of Wigston’s past. A selection of horrible histories, ghost stories, facts and phenomena. Hear about:

* Grisly Ghosts
* Medieval Murder
* Asylum Antics
* Pains of Purgatory
* Paranormal Places

The Roman Curse & other Tales (from historic Leicester)

Discover murder and intrigue around the West Bridge of Leicester.The walk takes you through the picturesque Castle Gardens, across the historic West Bridge, through the birthplace of industrial Leicester and culminates at the Roman ruins for a humorous Whodunnit ! 90 minutes historic guided walk around the West Bridge, Leicester. Meet at The Castle Gardens Entrance (Newarke End),The Newarke, Leicester LE2 7BY.Hear about:

* Monstours Murders
* Vicious Vigilantes
* Heathenish Vanity
* The body Snatchers
* The Anchoress
* Medieval Muck Lovers
* The Roman Whodunnit
