Why Tour with Me

Although it seems that it is only the discovery of the remains of Richard III that has raised Leicester’s profile as a City with historical value, all locals will know Leicester has a very long and very remarkable past. But it’s not just the City of Leicester that can offer great history and anecdotes. The surrounding suburbs hold many hidden gems from long ago - all within a stone’s throw of Leicester and easily accessible.


As a tour guide I take responsibility for showing you the best of Leicester and surrounding areas. All routes are carefully selected for their historical and significant interest. All tours last approximately 90 minutes and are relayed in an easy to understand, humorous manner, suitable for all ages.

I value each and every customer and I genuinely want you to enjoy your tour. It is with this in mind, I have created ways to keep you entertained and involved throughout. This includes the 'Lord's Pouch' which is brimmed with sweets and chocolates to encourage your participation. Most tours also offered the chance to sit and refresh yourself at a local museum or business and discuss what you’ve seen and heard before heading off.

Of course the most important factor is that you have fun, meet new friends, learn about the local area and hopefully come again!
