About Me

Born in Wigston Fields, I have spent my whole life in Leicestershire; local schools, local jobs and of course I am well acquainted with the local lingo, me duck!

My love of history began at an early age with school holidays spent at museums, stately homes and castles around the Country. It was this love of history that spurred me on to become a Volunteer Tour Guide at the Newarke Houses Museum in Leicester. It was then that I undertook formal Tour Guide training. The intensive course involved written and practical exams at the end of which I qualified as an Accredited Leicestershire Tour Guide and haven't looked back since.


Researching, writing and conducting tours has led me to meet some cherished friends including local historians and the much respected Alderman Duncan Lucas, founder of the Wigston Historical Society.

As well as working as a full time PA, I am qualified in Reflexology, Facilitating Learning, and an experienced Adult Education Tutor.

Other pastimes? Apart from my love of history, is a passion for animals, my beloved pet cat Sweep, Classic Literature, my partner Derek and quizzing.
